### EMAIL 9 · Day 28 · What Andromeda Rewards

**Subject**: `what the algo wants now`
**Preheader**: `the 3 signals that move spend faster`

**Body**:
```
{{first_name}},

After watching Andromeda behave across multiple accounts, here's what it consistently rewards:

1. Hook variation - Same product, 5 different hooks. The algo learns which voice/angle fits which segment of your broad audience.

2. Format diversity - UGC selfie, talking head, text-on-screen, B-roll. Each format reaches a different micro-segment of your audience.

The opposite (1 hook, 1 format, killed at $100 spend) is what most accounts default to. That's why most accounts plateau.
```
